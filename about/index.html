About

I am currently a graduate student in the Department of Physics at Ashoka University. I was earlier also a Teaching Fellow, and subsequently a Research Assistant, there for four years.

Before my time at Ashoka, I completed my undergraduate degree from St. Stephen's College, Delhi University, and my master's degrees from both the Ecole Polytechnique and the Ecole Normale Superieure in Paris.
